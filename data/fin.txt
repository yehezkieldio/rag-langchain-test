# Mirae Standard Operating Procedure: Expense Reimbursement

**Document ID:** SOP-FIN-002
**Version:** 2.0
**Effective Date:** 2025-03-15
**Review Date:** 2026-03-15

**Purpose:**
To standardize the process for employees to submit and receive reimbursement for business-related expenses.

**Scope:**
All employees incurring approved business expenses on behalf of Mirae.

**Procedure:**

1.  **Incur Expense:** Employee incurs an approved business expense.
2.  **Gather Documentation:** Retain original receipts and supporting documentation.
3.  **Complete Expense Report:** Fill out the digital expense report form via the Mirae internal system.
4.  **Attach Documentation:** Scan and attach all required documentation to the expense report.
5.  **Submit for Approval:** Submit the completed expense report to the designated manager for approval.
6.  **Manager Review:** Manager reviews the report and documentation, approves or rejects.
7.  **Finance Processing:** Approved reports are sent to the Finance department for processing.
8.  **Reimbursement:** Reimbursement is processed via direct deposit within 5-7 business days of Finance approval.

**Responsibilities:**
*   Employees: Submitting complete and accurate expense reports with documentation.
*   Managers: Reviewing and approving eligible expenses.
*   Finance Department: Processing approved reimbursements.

**Maximum Submission Time:** Expense reports must be submitted within 30 days of the expense date.
